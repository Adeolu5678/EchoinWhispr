### Persona 1: Alex (The Ambitious Founder)
**Description:** A talented developer with a great idea but no network. Needs funding and a co-founder.
**Goal:** To launch a project, find a co-founder, and get seed funding.
**User Stories:**
- "As Alex, I want to create a beautiful project page so that investors take me seriously."
- "As Alex, I want to post a 'Micro-Bounty' to find a designer for my logo."
- "As Alex, I want to update my 'Milestones' to show traction to my followers."

### Persona 2: Sarah (The Angel Investor)
**Description:** A busy executive looking to invest small checks ($10k-$50k) in high-potential tech startups.
**Goal:** To find vetted deal flow without sifting through noise.
**User Stories:**
- "As Sarah, I want to filter projects by 'Fintech' and 'Seed Stage' to find relevant deals."
- "As Sarah, I want to 'Soft Circle' a commitment to show interest without legal binding."
- "As Sarah, I want to chat directly with the founder to ask due diligence questions."

### Persona 3: Michael (The Talented Freelancer)
**Description:** A senior engineer looking for side projects or a potential CTO role.
**Goal:** To earn money/equity and build reputation.
**User Stories:**
- "As Michael, I want to browse 'Bounties' to find paid tasks."
- "As Michael, I want to apply for a 'Co-founder' role on an interesting project."
- "As Michael, I want to get 'Vouched' by previous collaborators to increase my trust score."
